Overview:
This is a Blackjack poker game.
It is played with four decks of 52 cards(total 208 cards).
Dealer stops hit at 17 or more.
Player CANNOT split cards in this game.

Features:
1. Record player information, including name, chips, times of win, lose and push.
2. Double, hit and stand.
3. When dealer shows an Ace, player can choose if he/she wants to insure.
4. Save & load player record.
5. Basic statistical information.

Algorithm:
1. poker deck shuffle algorithm: random switch two poker cards' place.
2. deal and record each card.
3. calculate total value of cards, assign Ace value based on current total value automatically.

Interface:
Player can interact with dealer(computer) through input of characters or numbers.
